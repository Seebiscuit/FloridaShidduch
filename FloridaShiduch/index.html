Frequently Asked Questions

Do you have to be born in Florida to be added to the database?

Our site is designed to find matches for individuals that were born or live in Florida. We do not restrict sign-ups to our database. We encourage them! However, if you do not live in Florida (we check your current mailing address) or you did not check the "I was born in Florida" option, our shadchanim will not be actively looking for matches for you. Of course, if a Florida single is matched with you, you will be contacted by an FSI member shadchan, regardless of your place or origin.


Is my profile public? Who can see it?

Your profile is not visible to the public. Only member shadchanim of FSI can see it. They will only send your resume to qualified matches. You may choose to have them contact you before your profile is shared with a match.


How do I become a member shadchan?

Please send us an email at members@floridashidduch.com. We will contact your to begin the registration process.
