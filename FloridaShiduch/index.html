FSI is a project dedicated to help observant Jewish singles find a match.

Our aim is to connect our Florida singles to other singles in their neighboring communities as well as the rest of the world. With the support of our Rabbonim, leaders and individuals from our communities, we will create and maintain an extensive database of singles living in or hailing from any Florida community. To increase the likelihood of a match, our database will also include singles from around the world, but will be optimized to find matches for Florida singles.

Our devoted shadchanim will personally get to know each of our singles and search for potential dates on an ongoing basis. The shadchan will contact each individual and his or her potential date to propose the match. No detailed personal information will be exchanged without the owner's expressed permission. All profiles will be stored securely on our site, being accessible only by our shadchanim, and singles will not have access to any profiles on our website.

At this time our service is completely free of charge. When a couple becomes engaged, the engaged couple would pay the shadchan through FSI as they would any shadchan, as has long been the custom.

How it Works

Application

Complete and submit our online questionnaire, as accurately as possible. All submissions will be kept confidential.




Interview

Once a shadchan finds a potential match, you will be contacted to set up an initial meeting. Ideally you will meet with you shadchan in person, though skype meetings are available.

Match

After getting to know you, your shadchan may propose a shidduch that is appropriate for you. You will have the opportunity at this point to research references and accept or decline the match.

Follow Up

Your shadchan will be there for you throughout the dating process to provide guidance and support as needed. Your shadchan will continue to work with you to help you find your perfect match.
